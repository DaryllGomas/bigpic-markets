# Equities & Sectors — Week 16 (Mon Apr 13 – Fri Apr 17, 2026)

*Snapshot date: Sunday, April 19, 2026. Includes weekend developments through Sun PM ET.*

> **Headline:** A historic risk-on week unwound the late-March/early-April Iran-war drawdown. The Israel–Lebanon ceasefire (Apr 16) and Iran's Friday declaration that the Strait of Hormuz was "completely open" sent the S&P 500 +4.5%, Nasdaq +6.8% (13 straight up days, longest streak since 1992), Dow +3.2%, and Russell 2000 to a fresh all-time high. **Then the weekend turned.** Iran re-closed the Strait Saturday in response to the US Navy blockade; on Sunday the USS Spruance fired on and seized the Iranian-flagged tanker *Touska* in the Gulf of Oman. WTI gapped ~+7% Sunday electronic to ~$89.74, Brent +5.8% to ~$95.59. Monday's open will face a mirror-image of Friday's setup: long crowded into cyclicals/tech, oil has just round-tripped, and the Iran "ceasefire trade" is now in question.

## Weekend Developments (CRITICAL for Monday open)

The cease-fire trade that powered Friday's rip is **partially unwound** as of Sunday PM. Sequence:

1. **Sat Apr 18** — Iran "fully closed" the Strait of Hormuz in response to the US blockade of Iranian ports, reversing Friday's "completely open" declaration. ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/4/19/iran-war-live-tehran-says-no-date-set-for-us-talks-hormuz-strait-closed))
2. **Sun Apr 19** — President Trump announced via Truth Social that the **USS Spruance fired on and seized the Iranian-flagged cargo ship *Touska*** in the Gulf of Oman after it tried to run the blockade. Iran vowed response. ([NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/04/19/nx-s1-5790378/iran-us-hormuz-closed-impossible), [CNN Live](https://www.cnn.com/2026/04/19/world/live-news/iran-war-us-trump-hormuz), [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/4/19/trump-says-us-seized-iran-flagged-ship-trying-to-get-past-hormuz-blockade))
3. **Sun PM electronic crude** — WTI May +~7% to **$89.74**; Brent Jun +5.8% to **$95.59**. ([CNBC — Oil prices jump after Iran/US attack ships](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/19/oil-price-iran-war-strait-hormuz-tanker.html))
4. **US-Iran talks** — Iranian sources had earlier indicated Monday talks would resume; Tehran is now saying "no date set." Trump warned the US would secure Iran's nuclear material "in a much more unfriendly form" if no deal is reached.
5. **IMF WEO (Apr 14)** lowered eurozone 2026 growth to 1.1% (from 1.3%), explicitly flagging Middle East energy-crisis risk. ([IMF WEO Apr 2026](https://www.imf.org/en/publications/weo/issues/2026/04/14/world-economic-outlook-april-2026))
6. **Other:** Trump nominated Dr. Erica Schwartz as CDC Director; visa restrictions tightened on workers from "U.S. Adversaries." Smaller magnitude vs. Hormuz.

**Monday-open implications:** The reflation/cyclical/Russell-small-cap rally is highly exposed if oil opens +7% and stays there. Energy/defense should bid; consumer discretionary (cruises, airlines that surged Friday) and the most aggressive tech long-streak names are at risk for mean-reversion.
