## Regulation & Institutional

**Regulation:**
- **CLARITY Act** (digital-asset market-structure bill giving the CFTC jurisdiction over most digital assets) continued advancing through Congress per CoinShares' James Butterfill — progress noted even amid the risk-off. ([crypto.news, May 26](https://crypto.news/bitcoin-sees-biggest-2026-weekly-outflow-as-crypto-funds-lose-1-47b-coinshares/))
- **Bank vs. crypto fight:** **JPMorgan CEO Jamie Dimon publicly criticized Coinbase's Brian Armstrong** and warned the current CLARITY framework "could ultimately fail," as banks and crypto clash over whether **stablecoin issuers can offer yield-bearing rewards** resembling bank deposits. ([via The Block](https://www.theblock.co/)) **[date not confirmed inside window — likely late May/early June]**
- **GENIUS Act** (stablecoin law, enacted July 18, 2025): additional implementing regulations are **due July 18, 2026**. Dec 2025 groundwork: FDIC proposed a stablecoin-issuance application rule (Dec 11), OCC conditionally approved 5 national trust-bank charters for stablecoin issuers (Dec 12). ([K&L Gates](https://www.klgates.com/Crypto-in-2026-The-Democratization-of-Digital-Assets-1-29-2026); [GENIUS Act — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GENIUS_Act))
- **SoFi stablecoin** launched (late May), with full availability "expected by early June." ([Investing News, May 29](https://investingnews.com/cryptocurrency-market-recap/))

**Institutional adoption (structural backdrop):**
