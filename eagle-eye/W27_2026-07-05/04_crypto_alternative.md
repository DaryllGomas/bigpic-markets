## Regulation & Institutional

**Legislation:**
- **CLARITY Act (Digital Asset Market Structure, H.R.3633):** Senate floor vote **expected before July 4 was BLOCKED/DELAYED** (Bitcoin Foundation / Elliptic, late June–early July 2026). Sticking points: DeFi regulation, stablecoin oversight, AML reporting, jurisdiction, ethics provisions, and Section 604 (law-enforcement objections aired at a White House Crypto Council meeting). Needs 7 Democratic votes to clear the 60-vote filibuster (assuming all 53 Republicans vote yes); Gallego and Alsobrooks called their committee support conditional. Analysts (Stifel's Gardner): to pass in 2026 it "probably needs to get through the Senate by the end of July." Emergency Senate meetings expected the week of July 6.
- **GENIUS Act (stablecoins, signed into law July 2025):** Implementing rules for USD-backed stablecoin issuers are **due July 18, 2026**, taking effect within six months (by Jan 18, 2027). Requires 100% liquid-asset reserve backing, AML/sanctions compliance, and monthly reserve disclosures.
- **SEC/CFTC:** Chairs Paul Atkins (SEC) and Michael Selig (CFTC) signed a coordination MOU on March 11, 2026 (historical context, not this week).

**Institutional / treasuries:**
- **~198 public companies hold ~1.268M BTC (~$77.5B)** as of early July 2026. **Strategy (ex-MicroStrategy)** remains largest with **>580,000 BTC** (~$60B+).
- ETF assets grew from $0 (Jan 2024) to **$130B+** by mid-2026; corporate treasury holdings ~tripled over the same window.
- **First major stress test of the treasury trend (CryptoTimes, July 2, 2026):** a growing number of smaller/newer Bitcoin-treasury entrants are exiting entirely amid 2026's drawdown and the AI-capital rotation. Newer vectors: RIAs/model portfolios adding 1–2% BTC sleeves; treasury strategies now spanning BTC, ETH, SOL and BNB.
