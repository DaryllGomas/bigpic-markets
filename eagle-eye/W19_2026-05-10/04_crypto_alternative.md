## Regulation & Institutional

### CLARITY Act — Markup Now Set for May 14
**Senate Banking Committee Chairman Tim Scott has scheduled the markup of the Digital Asset Market CLARITY Act for Thursday May 14, 2026**, with a target to bring it to the Senate floor in June/July ([Crypto.news](https://crypto.news/coinbase-pushes-clarity-act-senate-vote-next-week/)). This is the **dominant regulatory catalyst** for the sector this week.

**Stablecoin yield compromise (Tillis-Alsobrooks, late April / May 1):** Bans crypto firms from offering yield "economically equivalent to bank deposit interest" but permits incentives based on "bona fide activities or bona fide transactions." Coinbase and Circle endorsed it; **Circle stock jumped nearly 20% on May 4** on the compromise news ([CNBC May 4](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/04/circle-jumps-16percent-on-clarity-act-compromise-that-preserves-stablecoin-rewards.html)).

**Headwind (May 9):** The **U.S. banking lobby rejected the stablecoin compromise** four days before the markup — banks argue it still permits de-facto deposit competition. Senator Lummis on May 6: "Every day we delay the Clarity Act is a day American companies consider building their future somewhere else." Coinbase VP Kara Calvert warns 60 Senate votes are needed and bipartisan backing is essential ([Phemex](https://phemex.com/blogs/banks-reject-clarity-stablecoin-compromise)).

### Treasury "Operation Economic Fury" — Iran Crypto Sanctions
- **Late April / May 2026:** OFAC sanctioned multiple Iran-linked wallets; **Tether froze $344M USDT on Tron** across two addresses (one held $213M, the other $131M) tied to the Central Bank of Iran, IRGC-Quds Force, and Hizballah ([Tether.io](https://tether.io/news/tether-supports-freeze-of-more-than-344-million-in-usdt-in-coordination-with-ofac-and-u-s-law-enforcement/), [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/policy/2026/04/24/tether-s-usd344-million-usdt-freeze-linked-to-u-s-economic-fury-against-iran-regime)).
