# Crypto & Alternative Assets — Eagle Eye Week 28

**Snapshot as of:** Saturday–Sunday, July 12, 2026 (crypto trades 24/7; current weekend prices reported).
**Trading week covered:** ending Friday, July 10, 2026.

> **One-line read:** Crypto remains in a defensive, Bitcoin-dominated consolidation. BTC is holding the mid-$60Ks (~$63.8K now) after a stabilization week off its 2026 low near $59K; ETH is pinned near $1,780 with the ETH/BTC ratio at multi-year lows; altcoins and DeFi are deeply depressed (top-5 alts ~60% below ATH, DeFi TVL down ~39% YTD). ## Regulation & Institutional

**SEC — "Regulation Crypto" safe harbor (in-window):** The SEC signaled it could propose its first major crypto rule **as soon as July 2026** — establishing safe harbors/exemptions for certain on-chain activities including DeFi and tokenized securities, easing startup fundraising. [CoinDesk, Jul 7 2026; Cryptonomist, Jul 8 2026]

**Market structure — CLARITY Act:** The market-structure bill awaits a full Senate vote ahead of an **August 7, 2026** deadline; failure to clear the Senate by then would likely kill its 2026 prospects. The Senate Banking Committee released its own draft in January 2026. [Congress.gov / DL News, 2026]

**Stablecoins — GENIUS Act:** Enacted July 2025; key **regulatory implementations are due July 18, 2026** (just ahead — a near-term catalyst for the stablecoin framework). [Congress.gov, 2026]

**Corporate BTC treasuries:** Aggregate public-company holdings topped **~1.26M BTC** after a record Q2 2026 (~**110,000 BTC** purchased, largest quarter ever). **Strategy (MSTR)** remains #1 at **~843,775 BTC (~$53.8B)** but, notably, **sold ~3,588 BTC (~$216M) in late June/early July** under a new selective-monetization framework — a rare sale that dented sentiment. Strategy's "42/42 Plan" targets $84B in capital raises. Others: Twenty One Capital ~43,500 BTC; Metaplanet ~43,000 BTC. [TronWeekly / The Block / Coinpaper, Jul 2026]

**Quantum note (context, not in-window):** Per the quantum thesis, ~7M BTC (roughly one-third of supply, incl. ~1.7M in P2PK) sit in quantum-vulnerable addresses; a **Coinbase Advisory Board report (June 8, 2026)** flagged ~20,000 exposed public keys, and March 2026 research cut the estimated qubits for a cryptographically-relevant quantum computer to <500,000 (20x lower than 2019 estimates). Consensus still puts a real threat 10–15+ years out. Relevant this weekend only insofar as a Bitcoin **BIP protocol debate** (a July 12 headline) keeps post-quantum/consensus upgrade discussion active; no imminent quantum risk to prices. [Quantum THESIS; Coinbase board via QuantumZeitgeist, Jun 2026; Yellow.com, 2026]
