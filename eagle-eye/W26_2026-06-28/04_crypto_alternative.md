## Regulation & Institutional

**Regulation:**
- **GENIUS Act implementation deadline looms: July 18, 2026.** FinCEN/OFAC comment windows closed June 9, 2026; agencies are racing to finalize rules (Treasury sb0435). If an agency misses the 7/18 deadline, the statute has no fallback. GENIUS classifies compliant stablecoins as **neither securities nor commodities** (bypasses SEC primary jurisdiction); OCC's proposed 12 CFR Part 15 sets a **$5M minimum capital floor** for new issuers. No major *new* SEC enforcement action confirmed inside the 6/22–28 window.

**Institutional / corporate treasury:**
- **Strategy (MSTR):** Sold 32 BTC (~$2.5M, executed May 26–31, disclosed ~June 1) to fund STRC preferred dividends — its **first sale since 2022**; taken as a sentiment shift. It subsequently **bought 1,550 BTC (~$101.3M)**, ~50x the sale. Holds **847,363 BTC @ ~$66,384.56 avg** (as of 6/22/2026) — now **above current spot**, i.e., the position is underwater.
- Broader adoption context (not in-window specifics): spot-BTC-ETF AUM ~$130B+; BofA's ~$3.5T advisor pool can recommend BTC ETFs; Vanguard reversed course to offer exposure; >3.5% of 21M BTC supply held on public-company balance sheets (mid-2026 framing — historical context, not a 6/22–28 event).
