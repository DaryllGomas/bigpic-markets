## Regulation & Institutional

### Landmark SEC/CFTC Joint Crypto Guidance (March 17)

The most significant regulatory development in crypto history: the SEC and CFTC jointly issued an interpretive framework establishing a five-part taxonomy of crypto assets:
1. **Digital commodities** (Bitcoin, Ether, Solana, XRP, and other major tokens)
2. **Digital securities**
3. **Stablecoins**
4. **Digital collectibles**
5. **Digital tools**

This replaces the SEC's prior "regulation by enforcement" approach, supersedes all prior staff statements, and applies prospectively (does not affect prior enforcement actions). The SEC is planning a formal rulemaking process including an "innovation exemption" for crypto firms in coming weeks.

### Other Regulatory Developments

- **CFTC Innovation Task Force** (March 24): CFTC Chairman Michael S. Selig announced a new task force focused on clear regulatory frameworks for crypto assets, blockchain, AI, and prediction markets.
- **UK bans crypto political donations:** PM Starmer announced an immediate ban on cryptocurrency donations to political parties (March 25).
- **NYSE + Securitize:** The NYSE partnered with Securitize Markets as the first digital transfer agent eligible to mint blockchain-native securities for the NYSE's forthcoming Digital Trading Platform (March 24).

### Institutional Activity

- **MicroStrategy (Strategy):** Now holds 762,099 BTC (as of March 24) with a cost basis of $66,384.56/BTC ($33.139B total). Q1 2026 was the company's second-largest quarterly acquisition ever at 22,400+ BTC. Most recent purchase: 6,455 BTC on March 27.
- **Coinbase:** Launched a tokenized share class of its Bitcoin Yield Fund on Base in partnership with Apex Group ($3.5T fund services provider), targeting mid-single-digit yield via options and lending strategies.
- **Coinbase + World (Sam Altman):** Launched AgentKit, a toolkit letting AI agents carry cryptographic proof of human backing via World ID, integrated with Coinbase's x402 protocol for stablecoin micropayments.
- **Morgan Stanley:** Filed with SEC for its own Bitcoin ETF (MSBT) on NYSE Arca.
- **Coinbase Research:** Notes a "second wave" of institutional crypto investors prioritizing yield over price appreciation.
