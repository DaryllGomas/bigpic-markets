The Verdict

Breakpoint is rarely a neutral event. In bear markets, it has historically marked local bottoms or capitulation points (2022). In recovery phases, it acts as a massive fundamental accelerator (2023).

Data indicates that trading Breakpoint requires identifying the macro-regime first: Is the market looking for a reason to sell, or a narrative to buy?
